What are the core beliefs of the church?
We are a Christian Baptist church, rooted in the belief that Jesus Christ sacrificed Himself for the forgiveness of all our sins. Through His death and resurrection, we find hope, salvation, and a promise of eternal life. We strive to live by His teachings, sharing His love, grace, and truth with everyone as we grow together in faith and fellowship.
What Should I Expect During a Church Service?
Our services typically include worship through music, a message from the Bible, and time for prayer. Everyone is welcome, and you can participate as much or as little as you feel comfortable. Services usually last about two hours.
