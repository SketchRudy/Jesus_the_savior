Welcome to Jesus the Savior MBC

We are a community of faith devoted to living out the teachings of Jesus Christ.

Our Story

Jesus the Savior Missionary Baptist Church was founded in 1990 with a single mission: to share the love and teachings of Jesus Christ with everyone. Over the years, we’ve grown from a small gathering into a vibrant community that spans all ages and backgrounds.

Our Beliefs

We believe in the saving grace of Jesus Christ, the authority of the Bible, and the power of prayer. We gather regularly for worship, Bible study, and fellowship, striving to live out our faith through acts of service and love in our local neighborhood and around the world.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To reach and disciple individuals through the gospel of Christ, encouraging spiritual growth and community impact.
